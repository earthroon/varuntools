# R2 Delivery Incident Playbook

Incident types: R2_OBJECT_MISSING, R2_BUCKET_NOT_CONFIGURED, GRANT_EXPIRED, GRANT_DOWNLOAD_LIMIT_REACHED, GRANT_DELIVERABLE_MISMATCH, PRODUCT_NOT_FOUND, DELIVERABLE_NOT_FOUND. Open Delivery Incidents, confirm productSlug and deliverable, compare product.manifest.json with generated delivery manifest, check R2 object existence, then use support escalation copy. Do not expose R2 keys, private paths, raw payment keys, or raw buyer emails.
